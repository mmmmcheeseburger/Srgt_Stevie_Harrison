The Roadmap

Detailed Battle Plan

Phase 01 · Now – 3 Months
Enlistment & Preparation
Lock in your MOS contract and begin conditioning your body and mind.
  • Meet with Sgt. Stevie Harrison weekly
  • Pass ASVAB — target 65+ score
  • 3-mile run under 28 min, 80+ push-ups
  • Complete MEPS physical examination
  • Secure Mechanical MOS contract in writing
  • Clear any legal or medical issues
Phase 02 · Month 3–6
Boot Camp — Parris Island / MCRD
13 weeks of transformation. This is where civilians become Marines.
  • Black Phase — Receiving & Shock (Weeks 1–3)
  • Red Phase — Core skills & weapons (Weeks 4–7)
  • Green Phase — The Crucible prep (Weeks 8–11)
  • Week 12: The Crucible — 54-hour ultimate test
  • Week 13: Eagle, Globe & Anchor ceremony 🎖️
Phase 03 · Month 6–7
MCT — Marine Combat Training
29 days of combat skills at Camp Geiger or Camp Pendleton.
  • Advanced weapons and tactics training
  • Land navigation and field operations
  • Patrolling, ambushes, defensive ops
  • Complete as a combat-ready Marine
Phase 04 · Month 7–10
MOS School — Technical Training
Mechanical MOS school at Fort Leonard Wood or Camp Lejeune.
  • Motor Transport Mechanic course (10–16 weeks)
  • HMMWV, LMTV, MRAP, 7-ton truck systems
  • Hydraulics, electrical, drivetrain, engines
  • Graduate with MOS 3521 qualification
Phase 05 · Year 1–4
Active Duty — First Enlistment
First duty station. Build career, earn ranks, deploy, see the world.
  • PFC → Lance Corporal → Corporal promotions
  • Possible deployments: Japan, Okinawa, Middle East
  • Earn ASE / OSHA certifications
  • Tuition assistance — college while serving
  • Apply for Sergeant (E-5) promotion
Phase 06 · Year 4+
Reenlist or Transition — Your Choice
Multiple paths, all leading to success. The world is yours.
  • Option A: Reenlist + advance to Staff Sergeant
  • Option B: GI Bill — college or trade school
  • Option C: Defense contractor ($80K–$120K+)
  • Option D: Federal Law Enforcement (FBI, DEA)
  • VA Home Loan — buy a house with ZERO down
